2.2 Identify Gaps

About this Phase

The next step, the Identification of Gaps within your existing curriculum, builds upon your prior assessment of thx existing curriculum’s status quo. Iterating through the different means of analysis shall provide you a deeper understanding of what passages of your curriculum find counterparts in the job ads and simultaneously reveal gaps. Examples of such gaps can e.g. been found in form of associated terms that have been linked in the job ads, yet are not included the existing curriculum.

Example: A concrete example stemming from the prototypical set of job ads analysed by the tool includes the selection of technologies, which are specific to the field of Data Science. While assessing the practical relevance of individual technologies, e.g. Python, as a trending programming language the different means of analysis will offer the user a broader view revealing technologies that are linked this specific language and commonly co-appear in the job ads. This allows the user to identify related technologies or trends that might not have been included in your existing curriculum.

Note: For the given prototype, the tool is assessing job ads from the field of Data Science. The applicability is so for only given for related programmes. Yet, the applicability can be expanded by means of integrating different sources from different fields of job types.

4.1 Communicate Results

About this Phase

Having revised and edited your curriculum, it is the fourth phase’s overall objective to communicate and evaluate your results. As of now, you have reframed the content and learning objectives based on the information provided by the tool and the data it crawled. However, this approach comes not without limitations, or some additional requirements that have to be fulfilled before actually deploying the new curriculum.

As a very first step, you need to communicate your results to your stakeholders. Respecting your communication plan, set up in the early phase of the CDP, you now have the honour to communicate the first draft of your new curriculum. It is important to explain the reasoning behind your revision and choice of contents, e.g. based on the topicality of certain themes.

Having spread your vision of the new curriculum, it is important to take into account the feedback that you gather from various stakeholders. This is the direct goal of the evaluation following in the next step.
